About Nykaa

Founded in 2012, Nykaa is India's leading destination for beauty and wellness. With a wide array of makeup, skincare, haircare, and personal care products, we aim to empower customers with quality products and trusted brands.

Nykaa is more than a shopping platform — it's a beauty community. Our expert advice, product reviews, and tutorials help users make informed choices and discover new trends. With over 2,000 brands and a seamless shopping experience, Nykaa celebrates every individual’s unique beauty journey.
